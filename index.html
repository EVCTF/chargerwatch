ChargerWatch.org is a project of the EV Charging Task Force (EVCTF), a Maryland-based volunteer citizen watchdog group, published in partnership with PlugInSites.org.

The EVCTF files public comments in Maryland PSC proceedings, testifies at bill hearings before Maryland General Assembly committees, and its members participate as citizens in the PSC EV Work Group. The EVCTF's founder has 14 years of EV driving experience and has been quoted in the Washington Post, Baltimore Sun, and Politico.
